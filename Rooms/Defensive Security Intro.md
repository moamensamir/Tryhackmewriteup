# Defensive Security Intro

## First Task

**Which team focuses on defensive security?**

Security team divided into 3 main teams:

- **Red Team:** Specializes in attacks and finding vulnerabilities in the system.
- **Blue Team:** Specializes in defending from any threat and monitoring the logs.
- **Purple Team**

So the one that focuses on defending is **the Blue Team**.

---

## Second Task

### Q1: What would you call a team of cybersecurity professionals that monitors a network and its systems for malicious events?

**Answer:** **SOC (Security Operations Center)**

---

### Q2: What does DFIR stand for?

**Answer:** **Digital Forensics and Incident Response**

And this team focuses on investigating after the incident happens to find the one who did it by tracing them.

---

### Q3: Which kind of malware requires the user to pay money to regain access to their files?

**Answer:** **Ransomware**
